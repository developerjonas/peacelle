Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your Peacelle piece. If it doesn't bring you peace, we're here to help.

Return Window

You have 14 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return or exchange.

Condition

Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached. We cannot accept returns on items with makeup marks, perfume scents, or signs of wear.

How to Return

  1. Email us at returns@peacelle.com with your Order #.
  2. We will send you a prepaid shipping label (deducted from refund).
  3. Pack the item in the original reusable bag and drop it off.

Refunds

Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5-7 business days of us receiving the return.
