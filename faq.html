How do I care for my linen Kurthi?

+
Linen loves water. Hand wash or machine wash on a gentle cycle with cold water. Avoid the dryer to prevent shrinkage. Iron while damp for a crisp look, or embrace the natural wrinkles for a relaxed vibe.
