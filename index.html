The Origins

Born from Harmony

Peacelle wasn't born in a boardroom. It started in our living room, born from a quiet conversation about the noise of the world and the peace found in simplicity. We noticed a gap: the traditional Kurthi was often too loud for the modern office, yet Western wear lacked the soul we craved.

She brings the creative soul—focusing on fluid silhouettes, tactile fabrics, and the way a garment feels against the skin. He brings the structural mind—ensuring every cut is precise, ethical, and built to last. Together, we weave balance.

The Peacelle Standard

Earth-First Fabrics

We exclusively use breathable cottons, linens, and mulmul. Fabrics that let your skin breathe and return to the earth kindly.

Slow Fashion Cycles

We don't chase trends. Our Kurthis are designed with timeless A-line cuts and neutral palettes to last years, not weeks.

Golden Hour Aesthetics

Inspired by the warmth of the sun. Our palette revolves around Amber, Cream, and Earth tones to bring warmth to your wardrobe.
