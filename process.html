Sustainability

Seed to Stitch.

1

Sourcing

We source organic cotton from rain-fed farms in Gujarat and linen from European flax fields that require zero irrigation. Every fiber is GOTS certified, ensuring no harmful pesticides touch the soil.

2

Weaving & Dyeing

Our fabrics are hand-loomed by artisan collectives. We use Azo-free dyes and plant-based pigments (indigo, madder root, marigold) to create our signature earth tones, recycling 90% of the water used in the process.

3

Conscious Tailoring

Every Kurthi is stitched in our solar-powered studio. We practice "zero-waste" cutting techniques, turning fabric scraps into accessories or paper packaging.

Our Promise

We produce in small batches to prevent overproduction. When a design is gone, it's gone.
